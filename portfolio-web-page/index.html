About Steven

After moving down to San Diego to attend university, I got a job at Starbucks in 2014. After seven years of being a part of this amazing community, I realize that my true passion comes from creative problem solving to help those in need. This is where I got the idea to turn to Web Development, so that I may eventually work with a team of like-minded individuals to make the lives of others easier.

Over the course of this year I have been working towards building an online community on Twitch in an effort to both entertain and teach things I have learned and to share my passion. Every time I stream I try to incorporate friends and family in an effort to bring attention to their stories and perspectives. I have projects in the works to highlight their unique voices and hope to give them a chance to represent themselves in ways they may not have had a chance to in the past.

I am just starting out in my Web Development course, so there is still much to learn here at Thinkful. As I continue to grow and learn, I plan to foster a community much like I have been a part of this last decade. Thanks for taking the time to learn more about me, and I would love for you to reach out if you have any questions or upcoming opportunities you would like to collaborate with me.
